Our Story

Andersen in Tanzania was established in 2012 by visionary leaders committed to building a firm rooted in professionalism, integrity, and client success. Over the years, we have grown to become a trusted partner for businesses across sectors, providing high-quality tax and advisory solutions.

A Genuine Client-Centric Approach

At Andersen in Tanzania, we place our clients at the center of everything we do. By combining deep local expertise with the resources of the member and collaborating firms of Andersen Global, we deliver solutions that are tailored to the unique needs of businesses and individuals in Tanzania. Our focus is on building lasting relationships, offering personalized attention, and ensuring that every client receives the highest standard of service and value.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional tax and advisory services that enable our clients to thrive in a competitive global economy.

Our Vision

To be the most respected and trusted professional services firm in Tanzania and East Africa.

Our Core Values

Best-In-Class

We aim to be the benchmark for quality in our industry and the standard by which other firms are measured.

Stewardship

We hire the best and the brightest and we invest in our people to ensure that legacy.

Independence

Our platform allows us to objectively serve as our client’s advocate; the only advice and solutions we offer are those that are in the best interest of our client.

Seamless

Our firm is constructed as a global firm. We share an interest in providing the highest level of client services regardless of location.

Transparency

We value open communication, information sharing and inclusive decision making.
